Research And Development Department


     "Parivartan marks a strong deviation from NGO as it also focuses on innovative designs to welcome technology among the backward community. Our area of innovation , scales from projects like generating & providing electricity and energy in the form of hot water etc. to the village using renewable energy resources in the vicinity, to designing of small but highly efficient innovative domestic products made from material available in village like clay utensils, cold storage, cooking devices using renewable energy/ cow dung etc. Apart from technology we will also aim at innovation in the field of creating occupation by promoting production of traditional products with soul of village like toys , decorative items , paper/jute bags etc. that will help in providing sustainability to the whole system.


Our first project under R&D Team is on Water Energy Nexus


We are not NGO, but Innovators.
