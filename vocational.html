VOCATIONAL TRANING


    "Vocational education programs have made a real difference in the lives of countless young people nationwide; they build self-confidence and leadership skills by allowing students to utilize their unique gifts and talents."

    Main Aim of vocational training team is to spreading Awareness in appropriate manner so that it can understand by all. In India, employment is biggest or root cause of social backwardness so we are also focusing on alternative method of self-employment for adult or family people, increasing interest of children toward education and also awareness people right.

    In Every remote and backward area Peoples do not know about their fundamental right and government policy so we will increase awareness level of people which will give them independence of think about what is right for their family and society. We will also work as link between many NGOs which are not present in that area and needed people. In reality there are many NGOs which work for training purpose and employment purpose.

     NGOs have been constantly working day-in and day-out to solve various problems concerning children, women, senior citizens, environment etc. link SAMMAN foundation, GOONJ, akshaya trust, smile foundation etc. Skills acquisition must be matched with confidence building, and by developing inter and intra personal skills that will assist young people to move into an organised structure where they will face situations they will not have come across before, enabling them to interact successfully with people from all sections of society.
