HEALTH AWARENESS


"Our greatest happiness does not depend on the condition of life in which chance has placed us, but is always the result of a good conscience, good health, occupation, and freedom in all just pursuits."

Health department of PARIVARTAN aims to increase health awareness among people. Its mission has its focus on most deprived people of the society and the most effective way to reach to them is through having a genuine and sustainable relationship with the community.

"We are dealing with the prime public health concerns of our society. We are focusing On conditions with a major impact on the poor and disadvantaged and we are working alongside, maximizing what we can achieve together."

Our Strategy lies in working in three main direction:

  • Develop the capacity and to be able to understand the health sector and enable them to either strengthen their existing programmes or initiate programmes in the health sector.
  • Strengthen community outreach and advocacy skills within to promote better quality of primary health.
  • Promote constructive engagement and policy dialogue on the issue of primary health care.
