PARIVARTAN



     This Social Initiative is started by IIT Rajasthan Community. We are working in the direction of making an educated and well informed society around us. It is an initiative to improve education level and awareness in the poor and deprived section of society through teaching , youth counselling and parent counselling.

     We will adopt a village which is very poor, literacy rate is very low, where basic facilities of life like health , water, shelter , education etc are not easily available.

OBJECTIVE:- To convert that village into a developed village in such a manner that it will represent an ideal model of developed village in 21st century.

APPROACH:-
At very first step, such an undeveloped village has to be recognized. Some government agency ( block, or TEHSIL) or non-government agency can help to find such village in jodhpur or its nearby.

     SURVEY:- Initially survey has to be carried out at different level to study
1)the factors which are affecting their lifestyle.
2)root cause of their poverty.
3) Geographical constraints.
4)culture or tradition of that village
5)psychological aspect of peoples.
6)health and education level of that village.
7)source of income and amount of income of people.

Data or information obtained from survey will be critically analyzed. Possible solution for each
problem will be discussed. On the basis of conclusion of survey, different approach will be followed to address the problems. Solution will be categorized in the following group.
1) Only Volunteer work is needed
2) Volunteer work along with financial or monetary help is needed.

    Simultaneously, governments scheme for development of rural areas and welfare of poor people will be studied and the responsible government agency will be requested to provide advantage of those scheme to the people who deserve that help.

    Separate strategy for welfare of child, adult or working people and old people will be followed.
1) CHILD:- quality education will be made available to them
2) Adult or WORKING PEOPLE:- Our focus will be to enhance the skills of working population of that village through small workshop or training. And also increase the employment opportunity for skilled labour through our network in city or provide them opportunity to earn by working at their home itself.
3) OLD PEOPLE:- We will try to ensure good health to old people and also try to shape their role as moral guard in the village so that unethical and unfair act in the village can be discouraged efficiently. They can also play important role in eradicating alcoholism and other narcotics uses in the village.

We are not NGO, but Innovators.
