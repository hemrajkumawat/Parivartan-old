Our Vision

   PARIVARTAN is a social initiative of students of IIT Rajasthan which focuses on helping the backward section , the physically disabled and the deprived children of the society .It also strives to create a sustainable and greener environment.

          Parivartan,as the name suggests it is to bring change. Change here means change in the thinking, change in the lifestyle of the indian villager. Sixty-five percent of the Indian population lives in villages. They are not aware for their right.
